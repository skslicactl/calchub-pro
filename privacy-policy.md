# Privacy Policy for CalcHub Pro - Think Differently

**Last updated: December 13, 2024**

## Introduction

ThinkDifferent Tech Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") operates CalcHub Pro - Think Differently (the "app"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identifiable information
- User account data
- Contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Usage analytics for advertising

### Information We DO Collect
- **Calculation History**: Stored locally on your device only
- **App Preferences**: Settings stored locally for app functionality
- **Currency Rate Cache**: Temporarily cached for offline functionality

## How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used solely to:
- Provide calculation functionality
- Cache currency exchange rates for offline use
- Save your app preferences locally
- Improve app performance

## Data Storage and Security

- All user data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers except for currency rate updates
- Currency rate data is obtained from third-party financial APIs
- We implement industry-standard security measures

## Third-Party Services

### Currency Exchange Rates
- We use external APIs (fxratesapi.com, exchangerate-api.com) for real-time currency data
- These services may have their own privacy policies
- No personal information is shared with these services

### No Analytics or Advertising
- We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar tracking services
- No advertising networks are integrated
- No user behavior tracking is implemented

## Children's Privacy

CalcHub Pro - Think Differently is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

## Data Retention

- Calculation history is retained until manually cleared by user
- Currency rate cache is automatically updated and cleared as needed
- App preferences persist until app is uninstalled

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- Clear calculation history at any time
- Reset app preferences
- Use the app completely offline (except currency features)
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@calchubpro.com
- Website: https://calchubpro.com/privacy

## Data Protection Compliance

This app complies with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Google Play Store privacy requirements

## Permissions Used

The app requests the following permissions:
- **INTERNET**: Required for currency exchange rate updates
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: To check network connectivity
- **VIBRATE**: For haptic feedback during calculations

No other permissions are requested or used.
