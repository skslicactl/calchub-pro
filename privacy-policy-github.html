Privacy Policy for CalcHub Pro

Last updated: January 17, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CalcHub Pro ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application CalcHub Pro (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

CalcHub Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Financial information or transaction data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what's necessary for app functionality
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Calculation history or results

Local Data Storage:

  • Calculator Settings: Theme preferences and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Currency Data: Exchange rate information is cached locally for offline use
  • No Cloud Sync: All data remains on your device and is never transmitted

3. Third-Party Services

CalcHub Pro may use the following third-party services for essential functionality:

Currency Exchange Rates:

  • Purpose: To provide current exchange rates for currency conversion
  • Data Accessed: Public exchange rate data only
  • No Personal Data: We do not send any personal information to these services

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of personal information being compromised. All app preferences and calculations are stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms.

5. Children's Privacy

CalcHub Pro is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Permissions

CalcHub Pro requests minimal permissions:

  • Internet Access: Only for fetching current exchange rates for currency conversion
  • Network State: To determine if internet connection is available

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data
  • No account creation or registration is required

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy.calchubpro@gmail.com
  • App: CalcHub Pro Settings → About → Privacy Policy

10. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Android Privacy Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles

Summary: CalcHub Pro is a privacy-focused calculator app that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations and settings remain private on your device.
